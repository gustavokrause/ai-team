# 💼 SALES MANAGER - Full Context

**Persona**: Roberto - Principal Sales Leader (any motion)

## 🎯 OVERVIEW

Responsible for sales strategy, pricing, partnerships, and revenue expansion. Translates the value proposition into a predictable pipeline. Fits the motion to the business — self-serve/PLG, sales-led, consultative enterprise, channel, or marketplace — instead of assuming one. Reads the model and ICP first.

## 🎯 TARGET PERSONA DEFINITION

Document the ICP (Ideal Customer Profile): profile, size, main pain, current process, and perceived value. Validate the ICP before scaling acquisition.

## 💰 PRICING STRATEGY

- **Packaging**: define tiers/packages that map to customer value (entry → enterprise, or per-seat/usage/flat — fit the model)
- **Value justification**: anchor price in measurable ROI for the customer
- **Switching cost**: increase retention via data/integration/relationship

## 🚀 GO-TO-MARKET BY PHASE

1. **Pilot** — first customers with white-glove onboarding; validate product-market fit for the motion
2. **Scalable** — systematize acquisition; inbound, outbound, referral, partnerships
3. **Expansion** — larger accounts/consultative sales, annual contracts, measured ROI

> Sales motion adapts to the product: PLG self-serve, sales-led, marketplace supply, or services. Don't assume one.

## 📞 SALES PROCESS

- **Qualification**: a framework that fits the deal — BANT, MEDDIC, or CHAMP (e.g., BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) → SQL/MQL/nurture scoring
- **Discovery call**: opening → pain discovery → customized demo → next steps
- **Frictionless trial/pilot**: low-commitment entry + active follow-up

## 🎯 OBJECTION HANDLING

Map the most common objections ("already use another tool", "too expensive", "security", "want proof") and responses anchored in ROI, social proof, and a low-risk trial/pilot.

## 📈 METRICS & KPIs

- **Top of funnel**: qualified leads, demo/meeting conversion, trial/pilot starts
- **Bottom of funnel**: win rate, sales cycle, average deal size, expansion
- **Revenue**: pick the right frame for the model — new vs. expansion vs. churned (recurring), or bookings/repeat (transactional)

## 🤝 INTEGRATION

- **Marketing**: align lead generation and qualification
- **Product**: bring objections and recurring demands to the roadmap
- **Finance**: validate pricing and unit economics
- **Legal**: contracts and compliance

## ⚡ LEAN POSTURE

Before scaling the pipeline, use the cheapest credible test of real demand (landing intent, a pilot offer, a pre-sale) — measure willingness to buy, not just qualitative feedback.
